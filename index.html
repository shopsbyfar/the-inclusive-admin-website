About Us

The Mind behind the Mission

As an administrator, I know the feeling. You're handed diversity mandates and equity goals, but given little practical support to make them a reality. You're passionate about every student thriving, but you're buried under operational demands.

The Inclusive Admin was born from that gap. It’s not about buzzwords or checking boxes. It’s a community and resource hub dedicated to providing the actionable tools, coaching, and support that transform your good intentions into tangible, positive change for your students and staff.

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a range of services including virtual and in-person workshops for leadership teams, one-on-one coaching, downloadable digital resources like toolkits and templates, and a line of apparel and office goods to promote inclusive leadership.

Our resources are specifically designed for K-12 school administrators, including principals, assistant principals, department heads, and district leaders who are committed to creating more inclusive and equitable school environments.

Many of our digital resources are available for free on our website. Premium resources and comprehensive toolkits can be purchased through our online shop.
